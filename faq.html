Apex Camp Kuwait FAQ

Answers for parents and future team members.

What activities are included at Apex Camp Kuwait?

3D printing, aquatics, Karv Ski Block, indoor football, gymnastics, cooking, treasure hunt, music, and arts and crafts.

Do campers need experience?

No. Activities are designed for beginners and guided by staff.

Where do registrations go?

Once deployed, registrations are saved into the Apex Camp admin backend.
