About Apex Camp Kuwait

Apex Camp Kuwait is built for curious, active, creative campers.

Our kids camp in Kuwait mixes movement, making, problem solving, and expression so every camper can feel brave and capable.

Safe by design

Structured, supervised activities help campers try new things with support.

Variety every day

Campers move between sports, creative workshops, STEM, aquatics, music, cooking, and group challenges.

Confidence first

The goal is participation, progress, and pride for every Apex Camp Kuwait camper.
