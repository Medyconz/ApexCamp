Connect your business number

  1. Click Open WhatsApp Web.
  2. On your phone, open WhatsApp Business.
  3. Go to Linked Devices and scan the QR code.
  4. Return here and open chats from submissions or the form.

WhatsApp Web opens in a new tab because WhatsApp does not allow its web app to be embedded inside another website.
